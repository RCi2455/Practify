Consent to the Placement of Dental Implants

Please read carefully before signing
The purpose and nature of the surgical placement of dental implants and any later reconstruction has been explained to me. Please initial each statement to confirm your understanding before signing below.
1
I consent to the use of local anaesthesia / intravenous sedation for the surgical procedure. I agree not to operate a motor vehicle or similar machinery for 24 hours after sedation.
2
I understand that although every care is taken, the successful outcome cannot be guaranteed, but that failure of a dental implant is an infrequent event. In the event of failure, further implant surgery will be offered as appropriate.
3
It has been explained to me that possible complications may include discomfort, swelling and bruising. Numbness of the lip and tongue may follow surgery to the lower jaw. Sinus involvement and nose bleeding may occur following surgery to the upper jaw.
4
I understand that where bone is found to be inadequate, it may be necessary to place a bone graft or bone substitute to improve the chance of success.
5
I understand that after surgery it may be necessary to take antibiotics to counter infection, and I agree to refrain from smoking and alcohol consumption as advised.
6
I understand that mechanical failure such as fracture or loosening of the dentures, crowns and bridges may occur, but that this is an infrequent event with dental implants. Further clinical attention will be required in this event.
7
Alternative methods of tooth replacement have been explained to me.
8
I have given an accurate report of my medical history, including physical and psychiatric disorders, current medication and allergies.
9
I understand that the success of treatment depends in part on my maintenance of good hygiene around the implants. I undertake to arrange regular dental examinations, cleaning and x-ray examination as required.
